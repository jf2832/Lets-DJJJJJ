LET'S DJJJJJ

Self-studying Platform for DJ Beginners

Lesson Objects

Let's DJJJJJ hopes to help DJ beginners learn DJ through a free, open-source curriculumn. It also supports as a instruction tool for DJ teachers in a real-time interaction.

    Students can learn
    • Learn the basic components and process of DJ through our adaptive curriculumn
    • Setup their own DJ devices under customerized instructions
    • Using interactive tools on website to practice even if they don't have a device
    • Share their progress and creative piece with peers

Student Page

The student page is divided in two main parts. On the left column we have the general curriculumn, which is made of set up equipments, video tutorials, and practice. Students are first asked to set up their DJ equipments in order to learn DJ. Then after watching the video 1 on setting cue points and song structure, they will understand the simplest idea of DJ. Then the practice stage, they will listen to one song and set cue points as the song goes on, which will be evaluated and given feedback(interactive functions not developed yet).

New feature will come up soon where students can practice on a virtual controller on the webpage without using a real device. This is to prepare them before practicing in real life.

On the right column, we have a community-based study where students can submit their music records to the community and check others' works. They can also comment and interact with each other.

The Community feature will come up soon.
